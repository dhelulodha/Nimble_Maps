About Conference

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence(ICOAI 2018)
- where you have the freedom to take wings.

2018 5th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence(ICOAI 2018) will be held during in Paris, France during December 21-23, 2018.
The aim objective of ICOAI 2018 is to provide a platform for researchers, engineers, academicians as well as industrial professionals from all over the world to present their research results and development activities in Artificial Intelligence. This conference provides opportunities for the delegates to exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face, to establish business or research relations and to find global partners for future collaboration.
Submitted conference papers will be reviewed by technical committees of the Conference and published in Journals.
